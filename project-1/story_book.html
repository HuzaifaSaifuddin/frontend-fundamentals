The Art of Discarding: How to Get Rid of Clutter and Find Joy - by Nagisa Tatsumi

This book is divided into multiple sections.

  • Initially the author describes about how we human tend to attract to new things and keep on buying new stuffs without thiking of its need.
  • Next she mentions different excuses we have to keep on cluttering things in our life & how they affect us.
  • Finally she mentions about how can we get rid of clutter in different way & find joy & space in our life.

Why I like this book?

This book helped me get rid of cluttered stuff in my life and take wise decisions when out shopping or buying food.
