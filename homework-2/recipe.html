LEMONADE

A cool refreshing Lemon Juice.

Tired from a energetic sporting event like playing cricketm, badminton or gyming. A sip to the bottom of LEMONADE, makes me feel so refreshed & i feel a little less tired and more refreshed. Having a good alcoholic session with friends later in the night. LEMONADE again comes in handy to reduce hangover.😜

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 Lemons.
  • 1 Glass of Water.
  • A Teaspoon of :
    • Sugar
    • Honey

STEPS

  1. Take A Glass Of Water(🚰).
  2. Add a Teaspoon Sugar &/or Honey(🍯).
  3. Squeeze A Lemon(🍋).
  4. Mix Accordingly & Enjoy(😋).

I prefer a sweet lemonade. Tastes Sweet, Smell Lemony & looks refreshing 😉.
